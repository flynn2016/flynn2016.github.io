About Me

Hi! I am Fan, a game programmer, developer and designer currently enrolled in the Game Science and Design master program at Northeastern University. I started making games after undergraduate study and have been working on several game projects ever since. Here you can see some of the projects I've had the opportunity of contributing to, and some of the Game Jam I participated.


My personal interest thrives on implementing one’s idea into running games. Making things work gives me the ultimate pleasure. I will always seek new challenges to improve my programming skills and embrace opportunities to work with people with different skillsets.
